🏏 How to Play

Welcome to F&F CricFans League — a season-long fantasy cricket game for friends and family following IPL {{ 2026 }}.

  1. Register & Create Your Team — Sign up, pick a team name, and select 11 players from across all 10 IPL teams within a budget of 100 credits.
  2. Pick a Captain & Vice Captain — Your Captain earns 2x points and Vice Captain earns 1.5x points for each match. Choose wisely!
  3. Earn Points — Your players earn fantasy points based on their real-life performances in each IPL match. Points are updated after every match.
  4. Make Transfers — You have a season budget of {{ c.season_transfers }} transfers. Use them to swap players between matches. Transfers are free before the season starts!
  5. Climb the Leaderboard — Accumulate points across all matches. The player with the highest total at the end of the season wins!
  6. Mini Leagues — Create or join private mini leagues with friends. Compete head-to-head within your own group!

🔄 Transfers

  • You have {{ c.season_transfers }} transfers for the entire season.
  • Before the season starts — unlimited free changes. Build your perfect squad!
  • During the season — each net player swap costs 1 transfer. Swapping player A for player B = 1 transfer used.
  • Transfers reset per window — swapping the same player back in a window does not cost extra transfers.
  • Unused transfers do not carry over between windows — use them wisely!
  • All team rules (overseas limit, role limits etc.) must still be satisfied after transfers.

💡 Pro Tips

  • All-rounders are gold — they earn points from both batting AND bowling.
  • Captain choice is crucial — a big knock by your captain can swing the leaderboard.
  • Watch the match schedule — use the "Next Match" filter in team selection to see who's playing next.
  • Keep an eye on overseas slots — you can pick max {{ c.max_overseas }} overseas players but they're often the highest impact.
  • Transfer smartly — you only have {{ c.season_transfers }} transfers for the whole season. Don't waste them early!
  • Economy matters for bowlers — a bowler with great economy earns bonus points even without wickets.
